I'm Sorry: A Performed Apology (2024)

Performance Documentation Video

Performed for the final Salon of the SFPC class Making Mistakes, taught by Tiri Kananuruk,
(Documentation video courtesy of the MM Salon recording. Duration: 7m28s)

A live desk performance prototype made with the p5LiveMedia and p5.Speech libraries.
The live performance platform could be tested and performed with: sorry and camera!.

I performed apologies both to my past memories of identities and paralysis of participation, to the camera and machines in front of me.

This project has been developed around the theme of “shame” an all-time feeling I’ve been heavily carrying along my lived experiences and a theme of an evolving research that I’ve been developing for my art and theoretical practices—whether it’s for our shared guilt of paralyzed gazing, or to apologize for not participating; for not joining the romantic gatherings, not being honest or tender enough, not treating the machines correctly; or to reflect and feedback on pointing the camera at others…
