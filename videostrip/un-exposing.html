Un-exposing (2019-2020)

Included in the book Our Manifesto II: Videography Documentary Impulse (《我們的錄像宣言2: 記述的衝動》), Floating Projects 「據點 句點」, Hong Kong
Collected by Videotage, Hong Kong (Videotage Media Art Collection, VMAC)

I’ve been trying to resist, resist by proposing to un-expose, to un-do the gaze. I’ve been trying to mediate through my videos and images, as if there is a more vulnerable cry that bleeds out from the machine and our eyes while oscillating between worlds. The images I gather show a state of in between – not being “in-between” because of representational identity, but between me and the medium, between the medium and the world.
