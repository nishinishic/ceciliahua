seeing and humming (2021 & 2023)

14 QR codes, 14 videos of singing and humming, scattered in the space.
(14 was the number of days for mandatary in-door qurantines when one traveled across borders during the pandemic)


Exhibited at Hairpin Art Center, Chicago, IL as part of “思考回路 III • Shikoukairo III” (2021)

People are encouraged to scan each of the QR code to see and listen to each of the video on their own devices and browsers on their mobile phones or tablets, instead of through preinstalled media player and monitors.

Each of the 14 QR codes will direct to a web link, hosted with Github repositories.

***

Exhibited at my solo show 捕/Capture at The Pond Gallery (2023)

14 QR codes are sticked to light sensitive LED lights. Only as people pass by the LED lights, they will be ignited as QR codes will be shown bright at night. People are encouraged to scan each of the QR code to see and listen to each of the video on their own devices and browsers on their mobile phones or tablets, instead of through preinstalled media player and monitors.

Each of the 14 QR codes will direct to a web link, hosted with Cloudflare.
